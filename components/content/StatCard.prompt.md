Impact statistic — large brand-green number with a caption, for impact strips.

```jsx
<StatCard value="9" suffix="+" label="Vocational programmes" align="center" />
<StatCard value="12" suffix="+" label="Admissions from age" onDark />
```

Props: `suffix` for a small trailing "+"/"%", `align` (`left`/`center`), `onDark` for forest sections.
