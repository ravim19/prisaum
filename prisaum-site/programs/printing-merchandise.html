Printing & Merchandise Training for Special Needs

A practical print-and-product workshop in Chennai where learners with special needs master print production and finishing — building a marketable, employable skill set.

Enquire about admission See all programmes

Our printing and merchandise programme gives trainees aged 12 and above a real production routine: preparing designs, printing, and finishing merchandise to a sellable standard. The structured, step-by-step nature of print work suits many learners well, building sequencing, quality awareness and pride in a finished product they can hold in their hands.

What trainees learn

  • Preparing artwork and materials for printing
  • Operating printing equipment safely
  • Merchandise finishing and assembly
  • Quality-checking and packaging products
  • Following a production process end to end

Who this programme is for

Learners (12+) who enjoy structured, hands-on production work — including those with intellectual disabilities, autism and slow learning abilities.

Outcomes & certification

  • A marketable print-production skill
  • Strong sequencing and quality-awareness
  • Pride and confidence from finished products
  • Pathway toward certification in handicrafts

Frequently asked questions

What kind of products do trainees make?

Learners work on printed merchandise — preparing, printing and finishing items to a sellable standard, with full supervision.

Is the equipment safe for my child?

Yes. All equipment is operated under supervision, with tasks matched to each learner's ability and clear safety routines.

What age can my child start?

The programme is open to learners aged 12 and above, following an initial assessment.

Does this help with employment?

Print production is a transferable skill; our career counselling team supports the path toward suitable work.
